# 🚀 Website Updates Summary

Here is a clear, easy-to-understand summary of all the exciting upgrades and fixes that were added to the website today. These changes focus on making the site much faster, more reliable, and giving writers powerful new tools to create beautiful articles.

## ✍️ A Brand New Writing Experience
We completely replaced the old text editor with a state-of-the-art, modern writing tool. 
* **Silky Smooth Typing:** Writing articles is now significantly faster and more responsive.
* **No More Lost Work (Auto-Save):** You no longer have to worry about accidentally closing a tab or refreshing the page! Anything you type or format is automatically saved in the background. If you accidentally close your window, just open it back up, and your entire article will be exactly where you left it—with your cursor placed at the very end so you can instantly resume writing.

## 🎨 Better Article Design & Layouts
We added powerful new design tools so writers can make their articles look like professional magazines.
* **Perfect Image Alignments:** You can now easily align uploaded photos to the Left, Center, or Right of the page. Even better, the text will beautifully wrap around your images seamlessly.
* **Line Spacing Control:** We added a simple dropdown menu that lets writers adjust the exact spacing between lines of text (e.g., 1.15, 1.5, 2.0). This makes long articles much easier and more comfortable for your readers to read.
* **Beautiful Quotes (Blockquotes):** You can now easily add custom background colors to your highlighted quotes and important text blocks right from the toolbar.

## 🛡️ Perfect Support for Old Articles
Switching to a new editor usually breaks old articles—but not here!
* **Zero Formatting Loss:** We built a custom bridge so that any article published using the old editor will look 100% perfect when you open it in the new editor. 
* **Smart Hindi Text Handling:** The old editor used special background characters to prevent Hindi words from awkwardly breaking in half across lines (e.g., preventing "सचीव" from becoming "सची-व"). We ensured the new editor strictly respects and preserves these rules, keeping your older Hindi text beautifully intact without you having to reformat anything.

## ⚡ Instant "Most Read" Section
We fixed a noticeable glitch on the homepage that was affecting the **"सबसे ज्यादा पढ़ी गईं" (Most Read)** section.
* **No More Flickering:** Previously, the Most Read list would lag, initially showing the wrong articles, and then awkwardly flash/refresh a few seconds later to show the correct ones. 
* **Instant Loading:** We re-wired how the website talks to the database. Now, the absolute highest-clicked articles of all time load instantly in that section the exact millisecond you open the website. No waiting, no flickering!

---
*These updates have made the website significantly more robust, crash-resistant, and user-friendly for both your writers and your readers!*
